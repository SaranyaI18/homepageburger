Protect your online presence
The ever-increasing presence of technology in our daily lives has created new challenges for businesses and individuals alike.

Cyber insurance can cover you or your business for technology-related incidents such as data breaches, viruses, network hacking, employee error, and more. Cyber Insurance can help cover the costs of business downtime, IT forensics, credit monitoring, and cyber extortion costs – even including ransom payments to hackers. It can also cover your liability to third parties if you fail to keep data secure, including claims against you for compensation, investigation costs, defence costs and fines and penalties.
